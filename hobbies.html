Coffee

Don't get me started... On my Facebook account, I administer a photo album filled with over 4500 pictures of all the good-looking kinds of coffee I have consumed over the past years. About 2/3 of them are homemade by me, the rest skilfully poured by Baristas from all around the world. I don't seem to tire of admiring their aesthetics and could easily spend hours in a museum gazing at coffee pictures - if there ever was such a museum.

Culture

Culture to me means anything intellectually stimulating, be it traveling, learning new languages or educating myself about arts, music or cultures other than my own.
